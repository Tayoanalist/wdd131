Overview

Site Name

Site Name: Lily’s Beauty Salon

This name was chosen because it reflects the business where I currently work. It is simple, easy to remember, and relates to beauty services, which are the core of the business.

Optional domain availability: lilysbeautysalon.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide customers with easy access to information about the salon, such as services, booking options, and reviews. It will help potential clients explore available services and make appointments online.

Scenarios

  • How do I book a manicure appointment at Lily’s Beauty Salon?
  • What are the salon’s operating hours and services offered?

Branding

Website Logo

A simple logo will feature the salon’s name in an elegant font to reflect the salon's aesthetics. Colors will align with the website's palette.

Site Map

  • Home: Salon name, welcome message, featured services, and reviews.
  • About Us: Team introduction and salon values.
  • Services: Details about nails, hair, and pedicure services with pricing.
  • Booking: Contact info, hours, and booking form.
  • Gallery: Salon photos and examples of work.
  • Contact: Phone, email, and address.
